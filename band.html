The Band

Four musicians. One mission. Maximum volume.

Nasty Trick is a four-piece rock band from the south of the Netherlands. Rooted in classic rock and fuelled by modern edge, the band delivers high-energy live performances packed with original music and a raw, no-nonsense attitude.

Whether performing at festivals, club stages, or private events, Nasty Trick brings the kind of live show that leaves audiences wanting more — heavy riffs, driving rhythms, and a relentless stage presence that commands the room.
